🌊 8th Annual Irish Sea Level Meeting

Maynooth University | Hybrid Format

This year’s Irish Sea Level Meeting brings together researchers to discuss recent advances in sea-level science and coastal impacts. Day 1 features a series of invited talks and presentations, while Day 2 provides hands-on, workshop-style sessions aimed at building tools and skills for sea-level research.
